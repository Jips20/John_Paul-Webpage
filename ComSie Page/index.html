History

The Department of Computer Science at University of Manila was established as part of the university’s expansion and response to the growing demand for modern, technology-based education in the 1990s. Under the leadership of Dr. Virgilio de los Santos, the university strengthened its academic standards and elevated its institutional development to meet national and global educational trends.

On February 16, 1994, the university formally introduced the four-year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program, alongside other new offerings such as Hotel and Restaurant Management. This initiative was aligned with the government’s thrust to enhance higher education and prepare students for the rapidly evolving professional landscape.

Since its establishment, the Computer Science program has been designed to equip students with strong theoretical foundations and practical skills in computing, enabling them to contribute meaningfully to technological advancement and national development. Through continuous improvement, the department remains committed to producing competent graduates ready to meet the challenges of the modern digital world.

Mission
